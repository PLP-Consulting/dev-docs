MAX <Glossary>Cohort report [cohort analysis]</Glossary> ([<UILabel>MAX &gt; Mediation &gt; Analyze &gt; Cohorts</UILabel>](https://dash.applovin.com/o/mediation/user_performance)) helps you optimize the entire lifecycle of a user.
You can make better-informed decisions based on accurate and timely stats that you can see in one place, instead of relying on misleading, high-level, aggregate data available in different dashboards or that you calculate manually.

You can see metrics that include <Glossary>retention [retention rate]</Glossary>, <Glossary>engagement rate [ad viewer rate]</Glossary>, and revenue per install—all tracked over time for your users.

<Callout type="tip">
To save time, for reports that you frequently run, you can use the <UILabel>Saved Report</UILabel> feature that is available in all reports.

MAX also offers <UILabel>Compare</UILabel> functionality with which you can catch the trends in your <Glossary>monetization [app monetization]</Glossary> stack on an hourly, daily, or weekly basis.
This way you don’t lose the chance to maximize revenue.
</Callout>
