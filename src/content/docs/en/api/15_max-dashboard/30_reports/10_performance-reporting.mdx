MAX Performance Reporting ([<UILabel>MAX &gt; Mediation &gt; Analyze &gt; Performance</UILabel>](https://dash.applovin.com/o/mediation/performance)) helps you to understand your <Glossary>monetization [app monetization]</Glossary> performance.
You can <UILabel>Filter</UILabel> data and choose from the <UILabel>View by</UILabel> options to create different views that help you to visualize more granular data.

![Performance Reporting. Select a Saved Report. Network, 7 Days, SUB. View by: County. Filter. Compare. Estimated Revenue. Impressions. eCPM](/images/performance-reporting-01.png)

<Callout type="tip">
You can export all MAX aggregated data in <Abbr>CSV</Abbr> form by clicking the <img style="display: inline-block; max-height: 1.5em; margin: 0; padding: 0;" src="/images/export.svg" /> button in each section.
</Callout>
