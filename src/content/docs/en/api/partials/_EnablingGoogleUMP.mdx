### Enabling Google <Abbr>UMP</Abbr>

#### Enabling Google <Abbr>CMP</Abbr> on AdMob Dashboard

For the MAX consent flow to display the Google <Abbr>GDPR</Abbr> form, you first must create and publish the Google <Abbr>GDPR</Abbr> message on the AdMob dashboard.
To do so:

1. Sign in to your AdMob account at [apps.admob.com](https://apps.admob.com).
   * Add your apps to the AdMob dashboard, if you have not done so already.
1. Click <UILabel>Privacy & messaging</UILabel>.
1. Click <UILabel>GDPR</UILabel>.
1. Click <UILabel>Create message</UILabel>.
   The <UILabel>GDPR message</UILabel> page opens.
1. Select the apps that you want to display your message:
   1. Click <UILabel>Select apps</UILabel>.
   1. Select the desired apps.
   1. Click <UILabel>Save</UILabel>.
1. Select the languages in which you want to display your message.
1. In the <UILabel>User consent options</UILabel> section, select <UILabel>Consent or Manage options</UILabel>.
   <Callout type="note">
   Do not check the <UILabel>Close (do not consent)</UILabel> option.
   </Callout>
1. In the <UILabel>Targeting</UILabel> section, select <UILabel>Countries subject to <Abbr>GDPR</Abbr> (<Abbr>EEA</Abbr> and <Abbr>UK</Abbr>)</UILabel>.
1. Click <UILabel>Continue</UILabel>.
   The <UILabel>Edit message</UILabel> page opens.
1. In the <UILabel>Message name</UILabel> field, enter a descriptive message name to help you identify the message later.
   This name appears only on the <UILabel>Privacy & messaging</UILabel> page and is not visible to users.
1. Select the <UILabel>Styling</UILabel> tab.
   1. Under the <UILabel>Global</UILabel> section, set the <UILabel>Secondary color</UILabel> to white (`#ffffff`).
   1. Under the <UILabel>Buttons</UILabel> section, set the <UILabel>Secondary color</UILabel> to gray (`#6e6e6e`).
1. Click <UILabel>Publish</UILabel>.
  
##### Customize Ad Partners List
<Callout type="note">
By default, Google might not show all of your ad partners in your <Abbr>GDPR</Abbr> message.
If you fail to include these networks, this could adversely affect your ad revenue.
Follow the steps in this section to ensure all of your ad partners appear in the <Abbr>GDPR</Abbr> message.
</Callout>

To customize which ad partners show in the <Abbr>GDPR</Abbr> message:

1. Open the [<UILabel>GDPR settings</UILabel>](https://apps.admob.com/v2/privacymessaging/gdpr/settings) page.
1. Click the edit icon (<img src="/images/edit-icon.png" alt="âœŽ" style="display:inline-block;"/>) under the <UILabel>Review your ad partners</UILabel> section.
1. Select the <UILabel>Custom ad partners</UILabel> toggle and then select all of the networks you integrated in your app.
   For Mintegral, select <UILabel>Mobvista/Mintegral</UILabel> from the list of ad partners, rather than <UILabel>Mintegral</UILabel>.
1. Click <UILabel>Confirm</UILabel>.
1. Click <UILabel>Save</UILabel> at the bottom of the <UILabel>GDPR settings</UILabel> page.
